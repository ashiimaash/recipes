Chai

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp tea leaves
  • 1/2 cup water
  • sugar as per taste
  • 4-5 crushed cardommon
  • 1 clove
  • 1 peppercorn
  • half inch ginger

Steps to follow

  1. Remove the skin of the ginger by using a spoon to scrape it off. Cut the ginger into smaller, thin pieces or matchsticks. Lightly crush the cardamom with a heavy flat surface.
  2. Bring the water to a simmer in a saucepan and add in the ginger and cardamom pods. Boil for 2-3 minutes so the ginger and cardamom release flavor and the water turns a yellow-ish hue.
  3. hen, add the black tea bags or 2 teaspoons of loose leaf black tea into the water. Steep the tea for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat and add milk into the saucepan. Stir the tea together. Remove the tea bags.
  5. Strain the tea into glasses through a fine mesh sieve. Serve hot with sugar to taste.
